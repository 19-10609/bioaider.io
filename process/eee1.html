School Fundraiser

What is School Fundraiser?

The School Fundraiser Program of the Borderless Medical Society is a program in which students directly hold a school fund-raising event with friends and club members and donate the donations raised through this program to Korea for the Borderless Medical Association. Become a future sharing leader through your own experience as a fundraiser!

School Fundraiser's story

Jeju Namnyeong High School Activita Club

Participate in school festivals and external flea markets to sell 'sil bracelets' and donate proceeds to BIO AIDER

MChocho High School Oh! Neulhaerang club

Donate donations collected during the vacation from street and terminal to BIO AIDER

Osang High School Leven Club

Published medical newsletters, sold on campus, and donated the proceeds collected to BIO AIDER.
