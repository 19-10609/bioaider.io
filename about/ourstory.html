OUR STORY : 1983~

WHAT WE DO

A project designed to provide children with educational opportunities and to provide clean meals and comfortable bedding in clean places. Volunteers are recruited to practice direct service on the spot, and domestic research institutes seek ways to save children without discrimination such as nationality, ideology or religion. BIO AIDER helps families and communities develop their own capabilities to improve child situations in the long term. We train children's guardians and community residents to manage pumps and wells for drinking water, while helping them manage their nutrition and disease management at home.
