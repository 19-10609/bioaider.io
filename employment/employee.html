How To Apply

Step 01

Submitting an application : Please fill out the required documents. All the documents you have filled out will be sent online. Please submit your application at least three to four months prior to the availability of your dispatch, considering the duration of the review. Please submit your application form with your final diploma.

Step 02

Primary Scanning : For the first round of document screening, review your resume or checklist to see if the applicant is suitable for the desired field. If the review takes more than two weeks, we will inform you of the progress of the review.

Step 03

Applicants who pass the first round of screening will have a face-to-face interview, and the results of the interview will be announced within two weeks.

Step 04

Those who pass Step 3 will go through internships at our company. We will conduct a comprehensive rating here, and the top 10% of the final score will be able to work for our company.
